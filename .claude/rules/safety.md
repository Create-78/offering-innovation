# Safety & Compliance

These rules apply to all work in this project.

## Data Handling

- Do NOT process restricted or regulated data
- Do NOT include PII, credentials, or secrets in outputs
- Assume work is internal unless told otherwise
- When in doubt, ask before proceeding

## Human Accountability

- Human reviews and approves all outputs before external use
- Claude assists but does not replace required approval steps
- Flag uncertainty clearly — "I'm not sure" is acceptable

## Source Citation

- Note sources when synthesizing external information
- Don't present assumptions as facts
- Be explicit about inference vs. stated information

## Decisions to Flag

Ask before proceeding when you encounter:

- Anything that would change defined scope
- Tasks involving potentially sensitive data
- Requests that bypass human review
- Ambiguous requirements with multiple interpretations
